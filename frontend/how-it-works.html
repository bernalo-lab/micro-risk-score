How It Works

RiskPeek is designed to make evaluating trust simple, transparent, and secure. Here’s how it works in 3 easy steps:

1. Provide Information

You can assess yourself or someone else. Simply enter basic details like name, country, payment history, and optional links to online profiles (LinkedIn, GitHub, etc.). If you’re assessing someone else, make sure you have their consent or are using publicly available information.

2. Generate a Risk Score

Our system instantly analyzes the data across multiple dimensions:

  • Identity Verification
  • Payment History
  • Online Reputation
  • Compliance and Sanctions Checks
  • Digital Footprint

Within seconds, you receive a clear risk score and a short explanation of the factors behind it.

3. Save, Share, or Download

If you’re a registered user, you can save assessments to your dashboard, download a PDF report, or securely share your results with others. All sharing is consent-based—only you decide who sees your information.

For more detailed insights, you can upgrade to a premium report that includes additional verification and recommendations.
