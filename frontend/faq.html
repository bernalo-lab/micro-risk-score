Frequently Asked Questions

What is RiskPeek?

RiskPeek is a platform for generating digital and financial risk assessments based on user-provided data.

Do you store my data?

Freemium assessments are not stored. Registered user assessments are stored securely and only shared with your consent.

Can I withdraw consent?

Yes, you can withdraw consent anytime by logging into your account or contacting us.
