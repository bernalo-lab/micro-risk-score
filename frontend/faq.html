Frequently Asked Questions

What is RiskPeek?

RiskPeek is a platform for generating digital and financial risk assessments based on user-provided and publicly available data.

Who can use RiskPeek?

Individuals, freelancers, and businesses can all benefit from understanding and improving their risk profiles.

What data do you use to calculate a score?

Our scoring combines identity verification, payment history, compliance checks, online reputation signals (like public reviews), and your digital footprint.

Do you store my data?

Freemium assessments are not stored. For registered users, data is stored securely and only shared with your explicit consent.

Is RiskPeek GDPR compliant?

Yes. We adhere to GDPR and other data protection regulations. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time.

Can I evaluate someone else?

Yes, provided you have their consent or are using public information responsibly. Misuse of personal data is prohibited.

How accurate is the RiskPeek score?

Our score is based on multiple verified sources and proven algorithms. While no risk assessment is perfect, we aim for transparency and reliability in every report.

Is there a free version?

Yes. You can generate a Freemium assessment without creating an account. Premium assessments include additional insights and historical reports.

How much does a premium assessment cost?

Pricing varies based on the depth of the report and subscription options. Please see our pricing page or contact us for details.

How do I withdraw consent?

You can withdraw consent anytime by logging into your account settings or emailing support@bernalo.com.

How secure is my information?

We use encryption, strict access controls, and secure infrastructure to protect your data. We never sell your information to third parties.

How quickly can I get a risk assessment?

Freemium assessments are generated instantly. Premium assessments are typically ready within minutes.

Can I improve my score?

Yes. Your report will include recommendations for improving your risk profile, such as verifying additional information or addressing inconsistencies.

How do I contact support?

You can email us at support@bernalo.com. We aim to respond within 1 business day.
