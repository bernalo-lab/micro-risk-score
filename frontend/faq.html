Frequently Asked Questions

What is RiskPeek?

RiskPeek is a platform for generating digital and financial risk assessments based on user-provided data.

Do you store my data?

Freemium assessments are not stored. Registered user assessments are stored securely and only shared with your consent.

Who can use RiskPeek?

Individuals, freelancers, and businesses can all benefit from understanding and improving their risk profiles.

What is the RiskPeek score based on?

We combine identity verification, payment history, online reputation, compliance checks, and your digital footprint to create a holistic risk assessment.

Can I evaluate someone else?

Yes! You can assess someone else, provided you have their consent or are using public information responsibly.

Can I withdraw consent?

Yes, you can withdraw consent anytime by logging into your account or contacting us.

Is there a free version?

Yes. The Freemium assessment lets you check risk without creating an account. For more detailed reports, you can register for additional features.

How do I contact support?

Email us at support@bernalo.com.
