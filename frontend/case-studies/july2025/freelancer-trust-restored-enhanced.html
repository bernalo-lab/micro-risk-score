Freelancer Trust Restored: How a Global Risk Score Helped a Designer Win New Clients

Published: July 2025

Overview

When a UK-based freelance designer set out to win a significant contract with a Singapore technology firm, she encountered hesitation from the client, who was unfamiliar with her track record and reputation. Using RiskPeek's Global Risk Score, she was able to validate her credibility and secure the project.

The Challenge

Despite a strong portfolio and years of experience, the designer faced skepticism about her reliability, identity, and payment security. The cross-border nature of the engagement heightened concerns around fraud risk and made the client reluctant to proceed without further assurance.

The Solution

To address these concerns, the freelancer created a RiskPeek profile and completed verification steps, including identity checks, payment history validation, and collecting verified reviews from past clients. She then shared her Global Risk Score with the prospective client in Singapore.

The Results

The client reviewed the comprehensive RiskPeek assessment and felt confident in the freelancer's credibility. This transparency accelerated the approval process, resulting in a signed contract within days and immediate onboarding. The freelancer has since reported an increase in successful international bids.
